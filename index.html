About the scheme

Run in partnership with The University of Law, the Legal Cheek Summer Vacation Scheme brings together aspiring lawyers for five days of immersive, in-person learning alongside practitioners from the UK's leading firms.

Each day runs 9am to 5pm and features a mix of workshops, presentations, written exercises and networking. Sessions are led by trainees, associates and partners from firms including Jones Day, Willkie Farr & Gallagher, BCLP, Foot Anstey and more.

Written exercises are set by ULaw, with model answers supplied at the end of the scheme. Students who attend all sessions receive a certificate of completion.

How it works

01
Register online
Sign up via the Legal Cheek website. Places are limited, so register early.
02
Attend in person
All sessions take place in person from 9am to 5pm each day, 8–12 June 2026.
03
Complete exercises
Written exercises are set by ULaw each day. Model answers are supplied at the end of the scheme.
04
Network with firms
Daily networking slots and a dedicated law fair on Wednesday 10 June give you face time with 20+ firms.
05
Earn your certificate
Students who attend all sessions receive a certificate of completion from Legal Cheek and ULaw.
